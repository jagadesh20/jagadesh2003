Nature's Retreat: Exquisite Stay at The Silver Sky Hotels & Resorts

Experience an unforgettable stay near Chikkamagaluru at The Silver Sky Hotels & Resorts. Ideal for both corporate and leisure travellers, our resort in Chikkamagaluru is surrounded by lush greenery, wild trees, coffee plantations, silver trees, and pepper creepers. The coffee plantation is part of the property, offering guests a complimentary coffee trail tour.
